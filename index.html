Echo

Echo is a virtual chatbot that can answer questions about the following 4 knowledge bases: Sonic Playbooks, 2024 BMW Product Knowledge, 2024 Ford Product Knowledge, or existing VINS in the Sonic Inventory.

Echo will only answer questions using the knowledge base you connect it to. There are 2 ways to connect Echo to the correct knowledge base. Please follow one of the methods below to select the correct knowledge base, or to switch the knowledge base during the chat if needed.

  1. Type the command 'Playbook, 'BMW Product Knowledge', 'Ford Product Knowledge', or 'VIN Search', to choose the knowledge base you want, and press send/Enter.
  2. Type the command ‘About me’ and press send/Enter, then click the button corresponding to the knowledge base you want.

Please keep in mind AI generated responses may be inaccurate or incomplete.
